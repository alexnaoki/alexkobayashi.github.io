About Me

Ph.D. student from the University of Neuchâtel.

My research is focused in enhancing the knowledge of the processes involving subsidence and Greenhouse gas emissions on the Seeland (CH). To address this challenge, I am developing a low-cost alternative for monitoring CO2 gas fluxes to increase spatiotemporal resolution of those emission. This approach will provide information to feed to a numerical model and gauge the efficiency of different soil managements approaches for the region’s sustainability.

My research interests include:

  • Soil gas fluxes
  • Eddy Covariance
  • Subsurface hydrology
  • Land subsidence
